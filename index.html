Our Story

Cleos Eatery™ was born from a passion for authentic Italian cuisine and a love for warm hospitality. Founded in Amman, we bring the soul of Naples and the romance of Tuscany to every dish. Our dough is hand-kneaded daily, our sauces simmer for hours, and our ingredients are sourced from local and Italian artisans. Whether it's a family dinner or a romantic evening, Cleos offers a cozy escape with wood-fired pizzas, silky pastas, and creamy risottos. Benvenuti a casa.
